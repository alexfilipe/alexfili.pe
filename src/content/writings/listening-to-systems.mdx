Rehearsal trains a particular kind of attention. You learn to hear the whole while still noticing one late entrance, one unclear transition, one phrase that has not quite found its direction.

Debugging often asks for the same split attention. The failing unit is important, but so is the larger musical line of the system: ownership, timing, state, and expectation.

## A Useful Kind of Patience

Good engineering taste is not slowness. It is the patience to notice what the system is already trying to tell you before adding another layer of noise.
